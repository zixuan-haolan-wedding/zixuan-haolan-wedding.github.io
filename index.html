How we met

With our friend Zoey's help, I first saw Antara in the Fall of 2008 when we started our undergrad. I was instantly attracted to her but was far too nervous to speak to her. I had told my friend I liked her and news had traveled fast and she soon found out. Her first reaction was "Who is Ram?" and she began searching for me in the class. She started to take notice of me and no matter how boring the lecture was, we never missed a class after that. After a good few months I finally plucked up the courage to ask her out and the rest is history :)
